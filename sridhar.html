Charity/Sridhar's farm

Sridhar's organic paddy farm

Suffered due to gaja puyal

Description:

Cyclone Gaja was a severe tropical cyclone that hit the southeastern coast of India in November 2018, causing widespread damage and destruction. One of the significant impacts of the storm was the destruction of paddy fields, which are an important agricultural resource in the region.Overall, the destruction of paddy fields due to Cyclone Gaja was a major setback for the agricultural community in the region and had far-reaching consequences for the local economy. It will take time and resources to repair the damage and restore the fields to a state that will allow farmers to once again grow crops and support themselves.
